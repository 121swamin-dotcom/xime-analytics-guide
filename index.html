Course Catalogue

Click any course to see tools, technologies, frameworks and learning outcomes.

All Year 1 courses are mandatory for the entire cohort. Year 2 electives are offered in Terms 4, 5 and 6. A minimum of 6 electives across Terms 4–6 is required to claim the Analytics major.

Track 1 — Pure Analytics

Your goal is an IT or Analytics role across any industry. Go deep into the Analytics domain, including courses marked Recommended. A typical combination is 3 Analytics electives per term + 1 from another domain.

Track 2 — Dual Specialisation (Analytics + Domain)

Your goal is a specialist role in Marketing, Finance, Operations or HR. Take 2 electives per term from your chosen domain and pair with 2 Analytics Mandatory electives each term.
